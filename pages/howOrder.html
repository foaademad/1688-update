How to Order How to Pay Goods Delivery Guarantees

How to order

To place an order on our website, you should:

1. Register on website.

You can search and select products on the site without registration, but if you decide to buy something, you must register. After that, you can place orders. This is necessary for delivering your purchase to you.

To register, please enter username, password and email address. You can also go through registration process using these social networks, if you prefer.


Important! Try to create a complex password with a combination of letters and numbers (at least 6 characters). This will protect your account.


Your personal account on the site will be available to you after registration. Here you can enter personal data for goods delivery, check information about your orders, their statuses, your account and personal discounts. You can also send a message to site administrators if you have any questions.


2. Choose goods for purchase.

Before you order, you should select items you like and add them to the cart. You can select items from online store catalog or from search bar of online shop. Then you need to open goods card and set necessary configurations: color, size and other characteristics. Please note that price of some goods can change. You can add goods to the cart now clicking «Add to cart» button. A confirmation message will appear on the screen.

You can leave a comment to the order for operator with details if there are any difficulties in choosing the right product characteristics.


Besides, you can add goods you like into “Favourites”. They will be saved in this section, and you can easily find them, watch them again and order them at any convenient time.


Important! Exact delivery cost is calculated based on the exact weight of your order. Purchased items are weighed when they are delivered to the warehouse in China and delivery cost is recalculated on the basis of obtained data.


3. Order

Open the cart and select all goods you want to order by clicking check mark next to them. Then click “Checkout” button.


Fill in all necessary fields then for carrying out goods delivery and click “Confirm order” button. You can pay it after that.


Note: you can use “Quick order” button in the product card. It will save your time making an order.


How to pay

You can pay the order by any payment method specified during order processing.

You can also deposit funds in your personal account and use it to pay your orders.

You can track money flow for all time using the online shop in «Payment History» section.


Goods delivery

When ordering goods you have to pay:

1. Selected item cost.

2. HOW DOES ONLINE STORE WORK?

  • Internal delivery — goods delivery from seller to warehouse in China. Paid separately, may be free.
  • External delivery — goods delivery from warehouse in China to the buyer.

You can choose any convenient way to deliver goods when ordering. Chosen delivery option will determine its cost and the time it takes to receive goods.


You can enter personal data for delivery (full name, address, phone number, etc.) in your personal account by registering on the site.

Note! It’s impossible to change delivery option or address in the placed order.

Guarantees

2. HOW DOES ONLINE STORE WORK?

  • We help searching and selecting goods, as well as providing all available information regarding online shop operation at customers’ request.
  • We guarantee that all funds transferred by buyers will be used for purchasing and delivering their orders.
  • We offer solutions for the exchange or refund to buyer’s personal account on the site if we find discrepancy in goods in the warehouse.
  • We are fully liable if, for any reason, the buyer does not receive the order.

Important! We are not liable if the buyer does not like purchased goods. We cannot guarantee colors conformity and we are not responsible for products quality. We are responsible only for our online shop operation and goods delivery to the buyer.
